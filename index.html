How to Score a Goal

Thank you for joining me. Today I will be providing you instructions on how to score a goal in soccer!

Simply put, to a score a goal during a soccer match means to put the soccer ball into the opposing teams goal, sometimes referred to as a net.

In today's example, we will get down to basics and ignore the opposing team for now.

What you will need:

  • A flat ground (grass, concrete, sand)
  • A soccer ball
    A ball made of leather or plastic, used in soccer games
  • A soccer goal

You have all that? Great! Now it's time to follow these simple steps:

  1. Place ball 10 yards away from the face of the soccer goal
  2. Take a few steps backwards
  3. Step towards the ball and kick the ball with your foot towards the goal
  4. Try not to miss!

You've done it! You've scored!
